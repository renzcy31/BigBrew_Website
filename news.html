WHAT'S NEW?

Big Brew Launches Its Newest Flavor — Pistachio Overload!

Big Brew continues to innovate with flavors that bring people together, one cup at a time. The brand proudly introduces its latest creation — Pistachio Overload , a delicious blend that captures the rich, nutty goodness of pistachio, paired with creamy textures, chocolate bits, and chewy pearls for that signature Big Brew experience.

Big Brew Launches Its Holiday Series!

Big Brew is bringing festive cheer this season with the launch of its Holiday Series — a special lineup of drinks made to warm hearts and celebrate togetherness. Each cup comes in limited-edition holiday designs featuring cheerful snowmen, Christmas trees, and other festive icons, perfect for capturing the spirit of the season.

Big Brew Launches Its New and Improved Strawberry Praf & Milk Tea!

Big Brew is turning up the freshness with the new and improved Strawberry Praf & Milk Tea! Bursting with real strawberry bits, this upgraded blend is the perfect balance of fruity sweetness and creamy indulgence — giving you that irresistible “so berry fresh” taste in every sip.
